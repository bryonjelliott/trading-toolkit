Position Size & Risk/Reward Calculator

Enter your account, risk, entry and stop. Get the share count that risks exactly your chosen amount, plus the 1R / 2R / 3R sell prices. Add a target to see its reward-to-risk. Works for any trade.

Sell levels

LevelSell priceP&L $Return %R

R = your risk per share (entry → stop). 1R means a move equal to your risk; a 2R target is twice your risk. Sell price for each level is where price hits that multiple. P&L assumes the full position.

How to use it

  • Shares is sized so that if price hits your stop, you lose your chosen risk % — no more.
  • 1R / 2R / 3R are common profit targets. A popular plan: take half off at 1R, move your stop to breakeven, let the rest run toward 2R–3R.
  • Aim for trades where a realistic target is ≥ 2R (reward:risk ≥ 2:1) so winners outweigh losers over time.
  • Account size and risk % are saved in your browser for next time.
